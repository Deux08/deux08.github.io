Role play games I enjoy

Brave Frontier

The only RPG game i play is Brave Frontier. This RPG is more focused on gameplay, their story is simple. The storyline is about an unnamed summoner and players have to compete every stage to access a new area. Players are allowed to bring up to 5 units in each stage with a "friend" unit in quest mode. Players make friends through friend request. Attacking units in a battle rewards the player with Brave Burst Crystals, and Heart Crystals. After an enemy is defeated, the player proceeds the next stage until the boss, which is required to go complete a level. After completing, players are rewarded with Zel, Karma and items, as well as acquiring units from each mission.

Game story writing

The kingdom

Once upon a time in the kingdom of Wessex. The queen fell into an enemy trap when a Wessex priest, known as Leofric, lured the queen into the hands of the Danes, the enemy of the kingdom of Wessex. Leofric is the King's right hand and younger brother. However, he is secretly working with the Danes, hoping to overthrow the king with the help of their enemies. Aethelstan, the son of king Edward, who has been suspecting Leofric's malicious intention, wants to investigate this case further with the help of his close friends since the king did not believe that Leofric is a traitor to the kingdom.

Main Character Development

Aethelstan

The character i will be focusing on is Aethelstan, the son of the king that aims to find more evidence about Leofric's malicious intentions against the king. If he fails, the kingdom will fall into the hands of his uncle instead of him. Aethelstan is a 18 year old muscular, tall and handsome Anglo-Saxon boy. In addition, the heir to the kingdom of Wessex. Apart from being raised to rule the kingdom, he was also raised around warriors, to be taught how to fight and around priests, to stay true to his christian faith. At the age of 15, he has slain dozens dane soldiers in his first ever battle, and has been a better fighter ever since. His goal is to make Wessex a better place and unite the entire english kingdoms into a single kingdom called England.
