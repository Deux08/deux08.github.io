Introducing bingo


What is Bingo?

Bingo aims to help users to pre-order meals and book seats from their favourite restaurants. We believe that time is precious and desire to refrain people from wasting their time waiting to have a meal.


Software features

  • Allows users to pre-order meals (dine-in or takeaway)
  • Allows users to book seats
  • Allows users to check how long will it take for seats to be empty
  • Allows users to view how long will it take for the meal to be served
  • Notifies users the time to leave their current location
